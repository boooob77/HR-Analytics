HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI
Overview
An interactive Power BI report analyzing HR data for 1,470 employees across 3 pages to identify attrition drivers and workforce trends.
Pages

Executive Summary — KPIs, overtime, education field, job level, years at company
Attrition Deep Dive — Attrition by job role, distance, business travel, marital status, environment satisfaction
Satisfaction & Engagement — Job vs environment satisfaction, work-life balance, job involvement, training sessions

Key Insights

Attrition rate: 16.1% — highest among entry-level and overtime employees
Frequent travelers attrit at 24.91% vs 8% for non-travelers
Employees with no recent promotion show significantly higher attrition
Job satisfaction averages 2.74/4 across all departments
