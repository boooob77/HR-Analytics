# HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

## Overview
An interactive Power BI report analyzing HR data for 1,470 employees across 3 pages to identify attrition drivers and workforce trends.

## Pages

### Executive Summary
- KPIs
- Overtime Analysis
- Education Field Analysis
- Job Level Analysis
- Years at Company

### Attrition Deep Dive
- Attrition by Job Role
- Distance from Home
- Business Travel
- Marital Status
- Environment Satisfaction

### Satisfaction & Engagement
- Job vs Environment Satisfaction
- Work-Life Balance
- Job Involvement
- Training Sessions

## Key Insights

- Attrition rate: **16.1%** — highest among entry-level and overtime employees.
- Frequent travelers attrit at **24.91%** compared to **8%** for non-travelers.
- Employees with no recent promotion show significantly higher attrition.
- Average job satisfaction score is **2.74/4** across all departments.
